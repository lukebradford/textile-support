About

Textile is a quiet, distraction-free place to write. Jot down thoughts, draft ideas, or keep a long-running journal — all set in the typeface of your choice.

Run into a bug, have a feature request, or just want to share feedback? Get in touch and I'll get back to you as soon as I can.

Privacy

Textile does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Your notes live on your device and are never sent anywhere by Textile.

There are no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.
