About

QUARC reimagines the classic falling-block puzzle with beautifully curved pieces. Every shape is built from concave and convex arcs — rotate, drop, and clear rows to climb the leaderboard.

How to Play

  • Tap left or right to move a piece
  • Tap the center to rotate
  • Swipe down to drop
  • Match curves to complete rows and clear them

Privacy

QUARC uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as app launches, session duration, and gameplay events. This data helps us improve the game and is processed by Google in accordance with Firebase's Privacy Policy. No personally identifiable information is collected by QUARC itself.

QUARC also uses Game Center for leaderboards, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
